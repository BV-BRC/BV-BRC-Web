BLAST

The PATRIC BLAST service integrates the BLAST (Basic Local Aligment Search Tool) algorithms to perform searches against against public or private genomes in PATRIC or other reference databases using a DNA or protein sequence and find matching genomes, genes, RNAs, or proteins. For further explanation, please see the BLAST Service Quick Reference and Tutorial.
