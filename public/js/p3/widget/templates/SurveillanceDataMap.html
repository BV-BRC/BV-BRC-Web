Information

The markers on the map below show the locations of the selected surveillance records. The number inside a marker indicates the number of records found at that location. Each marker can be clicked to provide additional details about the records at that location, including the percent positive samples at that location. Be aware that the percent of flu-positive samples may be affected by sampling and/or reporting bias. When more than 20 records are found at a single location, a summary of the records is given.


The controls on the map can be used to zoom in or out or switch to a satellite view. The map can be dragged to move to navigate to different areas
